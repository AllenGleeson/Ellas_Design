About me

My name is Ella, I’m studying Textile Surface Design for fashion in NCAD. I love colour and try to incorporate as much colour as I can in my work. I’m selling my designs as scarves and bandanas. I would say my aesthetic is playful, pastel and girly! Most of my work is inspired by nature. At the moment my designs are available in Om Diva Boutique on Drury St.
